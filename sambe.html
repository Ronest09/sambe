What is Data Science?

Data Science is the process of analyzing data to extract meaningful insights. It combines various fields such as statistics, programming, and machine learning to understand and make predictions from data.

Key Steps in Data Science

  • Data Collection – Gathering data from different sources.
  • Data Cleaning – Removing errors and inconsistencies in data.
  • Data Analysis – Identifying patterns and trends in the data.
  • Machine Learning – Training models to make predictions.
  • Data Visualization – Presenting data using charts and graphs.

Applications of Data Science

Data Science is used in various industries, including:

  • Healthcare – Predicting diseases and improving treatments.
  • Finance – Fraud detection and risk analysis.
  • Retail – Personalized recommendations for customers.
  • Sports – Analyzing player performance and game strategies.

Why is Data Science Important?

Data Science helps businesses and individuals make **better decisions** by using data-driven insights. It enables automation, efficiency, and innovation across different fields.
